Design & Computation

I first took a programming class in my senior year of highschool and have loved exploring it since then. This site and and the examples below were created with html, javascript, and css, using Bootstrap and Figma. I have also had experience with object-oriented java programming and some C. Most importantly, I am always excited to learn more!


I explored the histories of 14 paper sons, 14 Chinese immigrants who bought illegal papers to move to the United States during the Chinese Exclusion Act. Many of these people were forced to lose and hide their true identities and their stories have been lost through time. These are the stories and identities I wanted to reveal with this project.

I created a data visualization project, running MPI program on a cluster of four nodes. I used python and pygame to create stars moving across the screen, each star representing a different person. A viewer can match a name to a star and learn details about their lives.
